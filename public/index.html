About Us

Building Excellence Since Inception

Vidyalankar Polytechnic has been at the forefront of technical education, providing quality education and training to aspiring engineers and technologists. Our institution is committed to developing skilled professionals who contribute meaningfully to society and industry.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry partnerships, we ensure our students receive comprehensive education that prepares them for real-world challenges.

Admission Process

  1. Eligibility:

    Passed SSC/10th with Mathematics and Science

  2. Application:

    Fill the online application form with required documents

  3. Entrance Test:

    Appear for the entrance examination or merit-based selection

  4. Counseling:

    Attend counseling session and document verification

  5. Enrollment:

    Complete fee payment and secure your admission
